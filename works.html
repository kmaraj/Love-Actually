"To Fall in Love With Anyone, Do This"

Psychologist Arthur Aron created a set of 36 questions that aims to curate a level of intimacy between two people. You and a potential partner can now ask each other these questions from the comfort of your own home with this site.

Follow the steps below to get started.

1

You and your partner will be asked to enter a unique password. It does not matter what this password is, just make sure you two enter the same password, so you will be brought to the same video call.

2

Click on 'Click to Start Video Chat' to start chatting with your partner.

3

The questions you and your partner will ask each other back and forth are on the right. Click the sliders on the carousel to navigate and take turns asking questions.
